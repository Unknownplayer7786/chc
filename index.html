About the Clinic

A quiet space for
thoughtful healing

Care Homoeo Clinic is a dedicated homoeopathy practice nestled in the serene Fakhri Hills locality of Kondhwa, Pune. Founded and led by Dr. Farida Dahodwala, the clinic blends classical homeopathic principles with contemporary approaches to cosmetology and wellness.

The practice sees patients across all age groups — from infants to the elderly — offering treatments that are gentle, non-invasive, and tailored to each individual's constitution and health history.
